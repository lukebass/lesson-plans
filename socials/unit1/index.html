    Unit 1: Where People Live and Why

    Lesson 1

    I Can

    • Explain why I would choose a place to live
    • Identify things people need to survive
    • Notice differences in environments

    Materials

    • 4 environment images

    Hook

    • Show images (BC coast, desert, mountain, forest/jungle)
    • Students choose where to live and vote

    Activities

    Think–Pair–Share - Why did you choose that place? - What makes it easier or harder to live there?

    Movement Activity - Students move based on importance of factors

    Key Concepts

    • Environment
    • Resources
    • Needs vs Wants

    Task

    • Draw ideal survival environment
    • Include food, water, shelter, resources

    Closure

    • One factor affecting where people live

    Lesson 2

    I Can

    • Identify survival needs
    • Explain importance of factors
    • Justify thinking

    Activities

    • Gallery walk
    • Compare drawings

    Task

    • Compare 3 environments
    • Identify resources and challenges
